This must be my favorite of all times! In Sydney's Australian Museum (great place), in the middle of the Pasifika exhibition (great exhibition), this huge touchscreen had somehow exited the interactive app that was on, only to display an Ubuntu homepage!

I was actually too chicken to try to do anything with the device except take a picture, although I could probably have restarted the app (you can see the Pasifika folder on the bottom right).

This also proves that mishaps do not only happen on Windows devices!
